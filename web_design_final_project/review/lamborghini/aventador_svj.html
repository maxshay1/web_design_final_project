Lamborghini Aventador SVJ Huracan Performante

Lamborghini

Written by: Maxwell Shay
Published on: December 6, 2018

Aventador SVJ

Stats:
• 0-100 km/h in 2.8 seconds
• 6.0 litre N/A V12
• Horsepower = 759 bhp
• Torque = 531 LB ft

Based on the original Aventador, SVJ is the outcome of Aventador SV and S being played on top of each other. This blend of Aventador variants is not just in the mechanics department, but in its styling as well. If one were to line up all Aventador models, it would be very obvious that Lamborghini’s design team likes to make their cars look more aggressive and track focused as the models evolve. When admiring SVJ it is very easy to see this progression towards increased performance.

The nose of the car now has a double front lip which features Lamborghini’s ALA active aerodynamics system for increased downforce at certain speeds. moving to the bonnet we see that SVJ features Centenario inspired louvres. These elements of its special, limited run sibling give the SVJ a much more menacing frontal look.. On the side of SVJ Lamborghini has widened the side air intakes for increased cooling of the internals. Lastly, the rear of the vehicle now has a Huracán Performante inspired exhaust setup, allowing for a shorter exit from the engine. Despite all of the aforementioned updates to the platform, the most noticeable change has to be the addition of a gigantic wing to its rear deck lid. This carbon fibre masterpiece conveys even to a non-car-person, the true intentions of SVJ: tearing up the track.
