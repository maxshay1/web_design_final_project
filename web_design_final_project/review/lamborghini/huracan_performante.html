Lamborghini Aventador SVJ Huracan Performante

Lamborghini

Written by: Maxwell Shay
Published on: December 6, 2018

Huracan Performante

Stats:
• 0-100 km/h in 2.9 seconds
• 5.0 litre N/A V10
• Horsepower = 640 bhp
• Torque = 443 LB ft

The track focused variant of the “entry level” Lamborghini Huracán - the Performante - is one of the fastest cars to come out of Sant’Agata Bolognese in recent years. In addition to being mind-blowingly quick, Performante is also stupendously intimidatory in the looks department. Beginning at the front of the car, Performante’s downward slopping bonnet makes it look slammed when observing it on the road. Furthermore, the designers’ unrestrained use of Forged Composites on the front splitter, rear diffuser, wing and throughout the interior makes it known that Performante is a special vehicle. Seen on Performante, for the first time in Lamborghini history is their cutting edge Aerodinamica Lamborghini Attiva (ALA), which is their active aerodynamics system that allowed Performante to set a lap record of 6 minutes and 41 seconds at the Nürburgring Nordschleife.
