Ferrari 812 Superfast 488 Pista Portofino

Ferrari

Written by: Maxwell Shay
Published on: December 6, 2018

488 Pista

Stats:
• 0-100 km/h in 2.9 seconds
• 2.2 litre turbocharged V8
• Horsepower = 710 bhp
• Torque = 567 LB ft

Ferrari has always gotten the track focused variant of their mid-rear engine V8’s right, 488 Pista is no exception. In terms of design, by taking a quick glance at Pista, one is able to figure out what Ferrari’s intentions for the car were. The side profile of Pista really makes the changes from the “standard” 488 GTB quite obvious. The nose of Pista has had a full redesign to include a new s-duct in the bonnet, as well as many vents and louver allowing for better aerodynamics and increased cooling capabilities. The mid section of the car has had only one notable update; the much larger air intakes for the powerful V8. Shifting to the rear of Pista, there is an much larger active spoiler adding to increased downforce, and acting as an air break when trying to shed speed quickly. Additionally Pista feature a more aggressive and sculpted rear diffuser and the Formula One style rear fog light which has previously been seen on the F12 Berlinetta, TDF.
