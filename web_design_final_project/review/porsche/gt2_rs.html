Porsche GT2 RS GT3 RS GT3

Porsche

Written by: Maxwell Shay
Published on: December 6, 2018

991.2 GT2 RS

Stats:
• 0-100 km/h in 2.8 seconds
• 3.8 litre turbocharged flat 6
• Horsepower = 700 bhp
• Torque = 553 LB ft

The widow maker. This has been the moniker of Porsche’s past GT2 RS models. Does it still live up to its name? Well yes, and no… Yes it has 700 bhp going straight to the rear wheels, but no, nobody has died yet. Taking after the other models in the GT series, GT2 RS has a massive wing seemingly borrowed from its cup car brother. It features a carbon fibre hood, and Porsche’s class 911 look. Taking the looks to the next level, GT2 RS looks like race car for the road. The front of the vehicle has been revised to include much larger vents, which allow for greater cooling of the brakes and other vital components. Similar to other Porsche turbocharged models, GT2 RS has large air intakes integrated into the rear hips of the vehicle so that the engine gets enough air to able to breath sufficiently. Lastly the exhaust tips exit from each side of the rear bumper to deliver an incredible sound.
