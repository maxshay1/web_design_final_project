Porsche GT2 RS GT3 RS GT3

Porsche

Written by: Maxwell Shay
Published on: December 6, 2018

991.2 GT3 RS

Stats:
• 0-100 km/h in 3.2 seconds
• 4.0 litre N/A flat 6
• Horsepower = 520 bhp
• Torque = 346 LB ft

The second iteration of the 991 GT3 RS, a naturally aspirated engine that revs out to 9,000 RPM at which point it is screaming its head off. In this update the GT3 RS has managed to claim the Nürburgring lap record of 6 minutes and 47.3 seconds which has since been beat by other manufacturers. From the front to the back, GT3 RS has received a refresh. The nose has taken on a new, larger front splitter for increased downforce. In addition the grill has a new shape for optimized air flow to the radiators. Furthermore, most of the exterior plastics have be swapped for carbon fibre for increased rigidity and styling appeal. One of the most visual changes to the vehicle has to be the addition of a massive decal on the side profile. The rear features a dual centre exit exhaust, which just happens to sounds incredible.
