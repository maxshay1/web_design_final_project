Porsche GT2 RS GT3 RS GT3

Porsche

Written by: Maxwell Shay
Published on: December 6, 2018

991.2 GT3

Stats:
• 0-100 km/h in 3.4 seconds
• 4.0 litre N/A flat 6
• Horsepower = 500 bhp
• Torque = 339 LB ft

Possibly the best drivers car to be released this year, available with a manual transmission or Porsche’s double clutch PDK system (one of the best available), 99.2 GT3 has stolen the hearts of enthusiasts around the world. Offered with a few configurations, GT3 is one of the most customizable vehicle to be released by Porsche in recent years. With the Touring Package, GT3 visually becomes “any” Porsche due to its lack of a massive fixed wing. With the “standard” GT3, it comes with the flamboyant fixed rear wing making it known to everyone around that “you have arrived”. Lastly, similar to GT3 RS, GT3’s dual tipped exhaust exits through the centre of the rear of the vehicle.
